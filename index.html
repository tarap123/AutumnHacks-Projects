Which CS Related Career Is Right For You?



STEM is such a broad field and can sometimes feel overwhelming when faced with the abundance of options. This is why we have created a website to combat this feeling and help people find the career which they are truly passionate about.



Navigating the website...



This website includes many interactive elements to help you find which CS related topic aligns best with your personality and interests-- after all, you know yourself best. Use the navigation bar at the top to go to the different sections of the website.


Click "Personality Quiz" to answer a few questions to determine whether you would fit best with a hardware or software related career.


Click "Resources" to learn more about different careers and majors you could pursue-- including the special feature Resume Screener. Additionally visit the links for some interesting scholarship and internship opportunities!
